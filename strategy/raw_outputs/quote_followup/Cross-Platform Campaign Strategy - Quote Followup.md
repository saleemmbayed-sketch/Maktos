# Cross-Platform Campaign Strategy: Quote Followup B2B Lead Gen Blitz

**Campaign**: "The Execution Gap" — Post-Quote Followup Automation
**Product**: Quote Followup
**Timeline**: July 15 – July 28, 2026 (14-day blitz)
**Budget**: $3,200–$5,000
**Lead Magnet**: "The Quote Followup Playbook" (gated PDF: 5 email templates + ROI calc worksheet + 7-day sprint checklist)

---

## 1. Channel Mix & Priorities

### Channel Ranking (Expected ROI for <$5K / 14-day blitz)

| Rank | Channel | Budget | Rationale |
|------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **1** | **LinkedIn DM Outreach (Sales Navigator)** | $0–$200 | Highest-intent touchpoint. Personalized DMs to decision-makers convert at 5%+ to demo. Cost is time + Sales Nav license. |
| **2** | **Cold Email (4-part sequence)** | $200–$500 | Scalable & measurable. 500–1,000 sends/day targeting VP-level titles. Reply rates target >3%. Best channel for volume + personalization combo. |
| **3** | **LinkedIn Sponsored Content** | $1,000–$1,500 | Targeted reach to VP Sales, RevOps Directors. Pain-point creative + lead magnet CTA. CTR target >0.8%. |
| **4** | **LinkedIn Retargeting** | $500–$750 | Warm traffic from landing page visitors + ad engagers. Case study testimonial creative. 3–5x better conversion than cold traffic. |
| **5** | **Organic LinkedIn Posts** | $0 | Daily thought leadership builds credibility. Supports paid + DM efforts. Repurposed to Twitter. |
| **6** | **Twitter/X Organic** | $0 | Shorter-form versions of LinkedIn content. Engage in #SalesTwitter conversations. Very low volume but zero cost. |
| **7** | **Niche Community Posts (Slack/Reddit)** | $0 | 3–5 communities (RevGen, Sales Hacker, Pavilion, r/sales). Tactical posts with playbook link. Low volume, high trust. |
| **8** | **Video (Loom)** | $0 | 3-min "before vs after" product teardown. Used in email + DM followups. Not a standalone channel — an asset. |

**Deprioritized**: Google Ads (too expensive for niche B2B at <$5K), Facebook/Instagram (lower B2B intent), Programmatic Display (brand awareness only, no budget).

### Channel-Specific Strategies

#### LinkedIn Strategy

| Tactic | Execution | Volume | Cost |
|--------|-----------|--------|------|
| **Organic Posts** | 1 post/day (Mon–Fri), 2 posts on Tue/Thu. Thought leadership (40%), educational (30%), social proof (20%), promotional (10%). | ~16 posts over 14 days | $0 |
| **DM Outreach (Sales Nav)** | Personalized DMs to VP Sales, RevOps Directors, Inside Sales Managers. Reference their recent post/company news. CTA: "Here's a playbook we put together on quote followup — might be relevant for your team." | 20–40 DMs/day | $0 (or $200 for Sales Nav) |
| **Thought Leader Ads** | Amplify 2–3 top organic posts with $100/day budget. Target 1st-degree + lookalike audiences. Use the post that gets best organic engagement. | 2–3 boosted posts | $600–$900 total |
| **Sponsored Content** | Single creative variant: "Stop losing deals in the quote followup black hole." Lead magnet CTA. Target VP Sales, RevOps by title + company size 50–5,000 employees. | 1 ad set, 2–3 creatives | $400–$600 |
| **Retargeting** | Retarget landing page visitors + video viewers. Creative: case study testimonial ("They recovered $24K/month pipeline"). | 1 ad set | $500–$750 |

#### Twitter/X Strategy

| Tactic | Execution | Volume |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Threads** | Convert top-performing LinkedIn posts into 3–5 tweet threads. Lead with stats (73%, 80% on followup #5). | 2–3 threads/week |
| **Quote Tweets** | Quote-tweet relevant sales conversations. Add data point + link to playbook (via link-in-bio). | 1–2/day |
| **Engagement** | Reply to VP Sales / RevOps tweets with value-add comments, not pitches. Build presence in #SalesTwitter. | 5–10 replies/day |
| **Pinned Tweet + Bio Link** | Pin top-performing thread. Bio link to playbook landing page. Organic engagement only — no auto-DMs (violates Twitter ToS + spam perception). | Always visible |

#### Email Strategy

| Sequence | Trigger | Emails | Goal |
|----------|---------|--------|------|
| **Cold Outreach (4-part)** | Manual upload of 250–500 target accounts | E1: Problem ("Are your quotes going dark?"), E2: Agitate ("The cost of slow followup"), E3: Solution ("Quote Followup in action"), E4: Social proof + demo CTA | Reply rate >3%, demo booking |
| **Nurture (4-part)** | Lead magnet download | N1: Deliver asset + quick-win template, N2: Case study ("One fix, 23% more closed"), N3: Self-assessment quiz, N4: Loom demo + "Book a call" | Convert downloader to demo |
| **Calendly in signature** | Every outbound email | "Want to see how automated followups work? Grab 10 min: [Calendly link]" | Passive conversion |
