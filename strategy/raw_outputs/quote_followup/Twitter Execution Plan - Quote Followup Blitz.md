## DAILY ENGAGEMENT ROUTINE

### Morning (30 min) — Reply to 5 VP Sales Tweets
Find tweets from VP Sales, Sales Leadership, RevOps discussing:
- Pipeline issues / deal velocity
- Sales process / CRM workflows
- Team performance / coaching
- Followup challenges

Reply formula: **Observation + Data Point + Open Question**
(Example: "We analyzed 200 deals and saw the same pattern — deals that got 5+ touches closed at 3x the rate. What's your team averaging for followup touches per quote?")

Never pitch. Never drop a link unless asked. Build presence.
