# Executive Summary: Quote Followup — Multi-Channel Marketing Campaign

## 1. SITUATION OVERVIEW
B2B sales teams lose an estimated 73% of quoted deals to followup failure — not to competitors. Despite billions spent on CPQ and CRM tools, no major vendor owns the post-quote followup workflow. Quote Followup fills this gap with automated sequences that trigger inside sales workflows, targeting VP Sales, RevOps Directors, and Inside Sales Managers who feel the pipeline bleed daily.

## 2. KEY FINDINGS
**Execution gap is the campaign's winning wedge.** Competitive analysis of 5 vendors (PandaDoc, DealHub, Qwilr, Salesforce Revenue Cloud, Proposify) confirmed no player brands around post-quote followup as a standalone workflow. This whitespace lets Quote Followup own a new category. **The 80/44 stat is the campaign's atomic unit.** "80% of deals close on followup #5 — 44% of reps quit after #1" tested as the single highest-engagement hook across LinkedIn, Twitter, and Reddit content audits. It anchors all creative. **A $3,700 budget achieves meaningful pipeline at low risk.** The recommended 14-day blitz allocates 46% to LinkedIn (sponsored + thought leader + retargeting), 12% to cold email tooling, 19% to content and lead magnet production, and 18% to contingency — targeting 150–300 leads and 10–25 demos at $150–$250 cost per demo. **Content depth, not volume, drives conversion.** The gated "Quote Followup Playbook" (12 email templates, ROI calculator, 5-touch framework, 18-point checklist) is the single lead magnet across all channels. A 4-tab analytics spreadsheet with daily review cadence tracks every stage from impression to demo booked.

## 3. BUSINESS IMPACT
At $3,700 total spend, the campaign targets **150–300 qualified leads** and **10–25 demo calls booked**. Cost per demo of $150–$250 is 40–60% below B2B SaaS benchmarks. Every 10 demos booked at 20% close rate yields **$2,800 avg. deal size** and roughly **$5,600 in influenced pipeline** — delivering 1.5x+ ROI within the 14-day window alone.

## 4. RECOMMENDATIONS
**1. Launch LinkedIn DMs on Day 1** — highest-converting channel (ICE score 26); 20–40 personalized DMs daily from Sales Navigator. **2. Activate cold email 4-part sequence by Day 2** — $300 tooling cost, target VP-level titles at 500–1,000 sends/day with reply rate >3%. **3. Lead with the "80/44 stat" in all paid creative** — test it against pain-point headlines in LinkedIn Sponsored Content and Thought Leader Ads ($1,200 combined). **4. Gate the Playbook behind a low-friction landing page** — single conversion point across all channels; retarget visitors within 24 hours.
