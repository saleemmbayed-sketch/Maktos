## 3. Channel Selection (3–5 Highest-ROI)

| Channel | Budget Allocation | Rationale |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| **LinkedIn (Ads + DMs)** | $1,500–$2,500 | Highest density of B2B decision-makers; targeting by title + company size + seniority is precise; DM outreach (Sales Nav) costs $0 and works; retargeting available |
| **Cold Email (Apollo/Instantly)** | $200–$500 | Scalable, measurable, cheap per touch; when combined with personalization + Loom video, reply rates hit 3–5%; can send 500–1,000 emails/day for pennies |
| **Landing Page + Organic Content (LinkedIn + Slack + Reddit)** | $300–$500 | Central hub for all traffic; lead magnet gating captures emails; organic posts in communities cost $0 and build credibility; serves as "always on" asset |
| **Retargeting (Linkedin)** | $500–$1,000 | Warm traffic from ads + organic converts at 3–5x higher rate; small budget goes far with LinkedIn Retargeting (CPC typically lower for retargeting) |

**Why not**: Google Ads (too expensive for niche B2B at <$5K, long-tail CPC too high), Facebook (lower intent, harder to target sales leaders precisely), Display ads (low CTR, brand awareness play only).

## 4. Conversion Optimization

### Landing Page / CTA Recommendations

- **Headline**: "Stop Losing Deals in the Quote Followup Black Hole"
- **Subhead**: "See how sales teams close 23% more deals by automating followups — inside Salesforce, HubSpot, and your existing CRM"
- **Primary CTA**: "Get the Free Playbook" (lead magnet) — orange/red button, above the fold
- **Secondary CTA**: "Book a 10-min Demo" — text link below asset
- **Trust Signals**: Logo bar (CRMs you integrate with), testimonial quote, "Used by 50+ sales teams"
- **Form Fields**: Name, Email, Company, Role — keep it 3–4 fields max
- **Social Proof**: "Join 500+ sales leaders who downloaded this playbook"

### Lead Magnet Ideas

1. **"The Quote Followup Playbook"** (PDF) — 5 email templates + 3 call scripts + weekly followup schedule template
2. **ROI Calculator** (interactive) — "How much revenue are you leaving on the table?" — computes $ saved based on deal volume + close rate improvement
3. **"The 7-Day Followup Sprint"** (Email course) — one email/day with a tactical followup tip + a template
4. **"Quote Followup Audit Checklist"** (1-page PDF) — 10-point checklist to diagnose their current followup process gaps
5. **Video Case Study** (gated) — 3-min Loom showing a real sales team's before/after results

### Email Sequence Concept

**Sequence**: 4-email drip triggered after lead magnet download

| # | Send | Subject Line | Content | Goal |
|---|------|-------------|---------|------|
| 1 | Day 0 (immediate) | Your Quote Followup Playbook is here | Deliver asset + "quick win" template they can use today | Deliver value |
| 2 | Day 2 | "We fixed this one thing and closed 23% more" | Short case study — one team's results | Build desire |
| 3 | Day 5 | How does your team compare? (Quiz) | 3-question self-assessment: "How healthy is your quote followup process?" + personalized score | Engage, segment |
| 4 | Day 7 | Want to see it in action? | Loom demo video (3 min) + "Book a 10-min call to see your own data" CTA | Convert to demo |

**Bonus**: Add a Calendly link in every email signature ("Want to see how automated followups work? Grab 10 min: [link]")

## Execution Timeline (1-Week Blitz)

**Day 0-1**: Build landing page + lead magnet PDF. Set up LinkedIn Campaign Manager + cold email tool. Write ad copy + email copy.

**Day 2**: Launch LinkedIn ads + cold email sequence (first 50–100 sends to test open rates). Post in 2 Slack communities.

**Day 3**: Analyze Day 2 data. Pause low-performing ad variants. Double down on best email subject line. Send Loom video to top 20 engaged prospects.

**Day 4**: Launch retargeting ad. DM outreach (Sales Nav) to 50 high-fit sales leaders with personalized note referencing their recent content engagement.

**Day 5**: Send email #2 and #3 to lead magnet downloaders. Reddit r/sales post goes live.

**Day 6**: Follow up on all DM conversations. Send email #4 to warm leads. Review all metrics. Book demos for next week.

**Day 7**: Campaign retrospective — what worked, what bombed, ICE re-score for next sprint.
