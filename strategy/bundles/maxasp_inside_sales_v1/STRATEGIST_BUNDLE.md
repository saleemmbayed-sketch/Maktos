# MAXASP Inside Sales Global Coverage Campaign v1

Status: draft, not approved, not executable.

## Executive Summary

This campaign targets industrial equipment and complex B2B manufacturers that may have global sales coverage gaps, incomplete decision-maker access, inconsistent follow-up, or CRM visibility issues.

The core offer is a practical conversation or diagnostic around extending global sales coverage with MAXASP's embedded native-speaking Inside Sales capability, supported by CRM Consulting and Data Intelligence where relevant.

## Campaign Objective

Primary objective:

- Generate qualified conversations with industrial sales leaders about global inside-sales coverage gaps.

Secondary objectives:

- Validate pain around regional coverage, follow-up capacity, CRM usage, and decision-maker data.
- Create a clean CRM handoff for interested accounts.

## ICP Segments

Primary segment:

- Industrial manufacturers with global coverage gaps.

Secondary segments:

- Companies with CRM visibility and adoption gaps.
- Companies with decision-maker data coverage gaps.

## Buying Committee

- Chief Sales Officer.
- VP Sales.
- Regional Sales Director.
- Head of Aftermarket.
- CRM / Salesforce Owner.
- Revenue Operations.
- Managing Director.

## Message Strategy

Core message pillars:

- Global coverage with local expertise.
- Embedded inside-sales capability.
- Better decision-maker access.
- CRM visibility and adoption.
- Intelligent revenue orchestration across identify, win, and grow motions.

Claims must remain evidence-led and approved before outbound use.

## Channel Plan

Primary channel:

- Cold email.

Supporting channels:

- LinkedIn tasks.
- Calling / inside-sales tasks.
- CRM handoff.
- Content proof asset.

Later channels:

- Webinars/events.
- Nurture.
- Paid amplification after budget approval.

## Cold Email Plan

Sequence intent:

1. Coverage gap hypothesis tied to industrial sales expansion.
2. Proof-led follow-up with global/local expertise angle.
3. Persona-specific angle: aftermarket, decision-maker data, or CRM visibility.
4. Polite close with opt-out and alternate-owner request.

Cold email must not launch from Strategy Studio. CampaignOps must own draft generation or validation, compliance gate, approval, audit, and controlled sending.

## LinkedIn And Calling Task Plan

LinkedIn:

- Manual profile research.
- Manual connection or engagement themes.
- No autonomous LinkedIn sending.

Calling:

- Human-led discovery for high-fit accounts.
- Use account trigger, persona pain hypothesis, prior email status, and relevant proof point.

## CRM Handoff Requirements

Required fields include campaign name, bundle ID, ICP segment, persona, source channel, lead score, compliance status, approval status, reply category, SLA owner, and outcome.

## Experiment Plan

Hypothesis 1:

- Sales leaders respond better to coverage-gap messaging, while CRM/RevOps buyers respond better to data and visibility messaging.

Hypothesis 2:

- A coverage-gap diagnostic offer creates more qualified replies than a generic discovery-call CTA.
