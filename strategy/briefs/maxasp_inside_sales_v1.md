# MAXASP Inside Sales Campaign Brief v1

## Campaign Name

MAXASP Inside Sales Global Coverage Campaign v1

## Target Solution

- Inside Sales

## Target Segment

Industry:

- Industrial equipment manufacturers and complex B2B manufacturers.

Geography:

- Initial assumption: DACH and European industrial companies with international customer bases.

Company size:

- Mid-market to enterprise industrial companies with distributed sales coverage needs.

CRM maturity:

- Companies using CRM but struggling with adoption, pipeline hygiene, regional visibility, or decision-maker coverage.

Sales motion:

- High-value equipment, aftermarket, service, spare parts, or project sales.

## Buying Committee

- Economic buyer: Chief Sales Officer, VP Sales, Managing Director.
- Operational owner: Head of Sales, Regional Sales Director, Head of Inside Sales.
- Technical/CRM stakeholder: CRM Owner, Salesforce Owner, Revenue Operations.
- Segment stakeholder: Head of Aftermarket, Service Sales, Spare Parts Sales.

## Campaign Goal

Primary conversion:

- Qualified conversation about expanding global sales coverage with embedded inside sales capability.

Secondary conversion:

- Reply, discovery call, or referral to sales/CRM/data owner.

## Pain Hypothesis

Industrial companies often have valuable customer and prospect opportunities across regions but limited local-language coverage, inconsistent follow-up, weak CRM adoption, and incomplete decision-maker data.

## Offer

Offer a short discussion or diagnostic around global inside-sales coverage gaps and how MAXASP can help identify, win, and grow revenue opportunities through embedded native-speaking teams and data-backed execution.
