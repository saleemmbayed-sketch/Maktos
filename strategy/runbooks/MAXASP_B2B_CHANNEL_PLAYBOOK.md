# MAXASP B2B Channel Playbook

This playbook defines how Strategy Studio should plan MAXASP B2B campaigns across cold email and supporting B2B channels.

It does not authorize execution. CampaignOps owns compliance, approval, audit, and controlled sending.

## Channel Principles

- Start with account fit and buying committee before choosing channels.
- Use cold email for controlled, measurable direct outreach.
- Use LinkedIn as human-assisted research, credibility, and task generation.
- Use calling as a sales-assist motion for high-fit accounts.
- Use content and webinars to create proof and warm follow-up paths.
- Use CRM as the operational handoff and measurement backbone.
- Use nurture only after consent, engagement, or approved lifecycle rules.

## Cold Email

Purpose:

- Reach specific industrial decision-makers with relevant business context.
- Validate pain, timing, and interest.
- Create qualified replies, meetings, or sales conversations.

Best-fit use cases:

- Target-account campaigns.
- Installed-base or aftermarket growth campaigns.
- Regional expansion campaigns.
- CRM adoption or pipeline visibility campaigns.
- Data quality and decision-maker coverage campaigns.

Required Strategy Studio outputs:

- Target segment and exclusion logic.
- Persona-specific pain hypothesis.
- Email sequence intent by step.
- Message matrix with claims and proof mapping.
- Personalization fields required.
- Compliance risks and unsubscribe notes.
- Human approval notes.

CampaignOps boundary:

- CampaignOps must generate or validate executable drafts.
- Compliance gate must pass.
- Approval queue must clear where required.
- Audit logging must record send eligibility and state transitions.

Cold email quality rules:

- No generic automation language.
- No unsupported revenue guarantees.
- No deceptive subject lines.
- No fake familiarity.
- Include clear opt-out/unsubscribe handling.
- Prefer short, industrial-context messages tied to role, account, trigger, or operational gap.

## LinkedIn Tasks

Purpose:

- Support account research, credibility, and human-assisted engagement.
- Create manual task recommendations for sales users.

Allowed Strategy Studio outputs:

- Profile research checklist.
- Connection-note themes.
- Commenting themes.
- Follow-up prompts.
- Account stakeholder map.

Boundary:

- No autonomous LinkedIn sending.
- No scraping instructions that violate platform rules.
- CampaignOps can generate tasks, not execute social actions autonomously.

## Calling And Inside Sales

Purpose:

- Prioritize high-fit accounts and convert interest into qualified conversations.

Required Strategy Studio outputs:

- Call objective.
- Account context.
- Persona pain hypothesis.
- Discovery questions.
- Objection handling.
- Voicemail theme.
- CRM disposition options.

Boundary:

- Calls are human-led.
- CampaignOps may create tasks and log outcomes but should not own call content truth outside approved playbooks.

## CRM Handoff

Purpose:

- Ensure every campaign can be measured and operationalized.

Required fields:

- Campaign name.
- Source channel.
- ICP segment.
- Persona.
- Account trigger.
- Lead score inputs.
- Compliance status.
- Approval status.
- Reply category.
- SLA owner.
- Opportunity or meeting outcome.

Preferred CRM integrations:

- Pardot / Salesforce.
- HubSpot where appropriate.

## Content And Thought Leadership

Purpose:

- Provide proof, education, and warm follow-up assets for industrial buyers.

Recommended formats:

- Executive insight post.
- CRM adoption checklist.
- Aftermarket coverage brief.
- Global sales coverage diagnostic.
- Decision-maker data quality checklist.
- Case-study-led proof asset.

Boundary:

- Content supports campaigns but does not bypass campaign approval.
- Proof claims must map to available sources.

## Webinars And Events

Purpose:

- Create high-trust demand generation around industrial revenue topics.

Recommended themes:

- Global sales coverage without building every local team.
- CRM adoption for forecast visibility.
- Aftermarket growth through decision-maker coverage.
- From outsourcing to intelligent revenue orchestration.

Required outputs:

- Target audience.
- Registration offer.
- Follow-up sequence intent.
- Sales handoff rules.
- Measurement plan.

## Nurture

Purpose:

- Continue engagement after consent, meaningful interaction, or approved lifecycle trigger.

Boundary:

- Nurture requires consent or a compliant basis.
- CampaignOps and marketing automation adapters own execution state.

## Paid Channels

Purpose:

- Amplify content to defined B2B audiences.

Allowed Strategy Studio outputs:

- Audience hypothesis.
- Creative themes.
- Landing-page requirements.
- Measurement plan.

Boundary:

- No autonomous spend.
- Budget approval is required outside CampaignOps MVP.

## Channel Selection Matrix

| Scenario | Primary Channel | Supporting Channels |
|---|---|---|
| Named account outreach | Cold email | LinkedIn tasks, calling, CRM tasks |
| CRM transformation campaign | Content | Cold email, LinkedIn, webinar |
| Aftermarket growth | Cold email | Calling, data intelligence, CRM handoff |
| Regional expansion | Cold email | LinkedIn tasks, inside sales, content |
| Data quality offer | Cold email | Diagnostic asset, CRM handoff |
| Executive awareness | LinkedIn/content | Webinar, targeted email follow-up |

## Required Channel Plan Output

Every MAXASP sprint must produce:

- Selected channels.
- Channel rationale.
- Segment/persona fit.
- Message intent by channel.
- Compliance risks by channel.
- Required data fields.
- CampaignOps execution boundary.
- Measurement events.
